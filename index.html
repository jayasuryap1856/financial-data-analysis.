Abstract

Problem Statement Abstract

Most financial information is locked up in huge amounts of unstructured data that are difficult to access, whether they are annual reports, balance sheets, or transcripts of earnings calls, because the information is only available to those who have the right expertise and tools to interpret it. This means that there is a major information imbalance between institutional investors that have proprietary analytics systems and retail investors or SMEs who do not.

To bridge this gap, we propose FinWhisper, an end-to-end automated financial data analytics platform that integrates NLP, Machine Learning and Large Language Model capabilities in one platform. Four core modules are developed: a Document Intelligence Module based on PyMuPDF/pdfplumber/spaCy for extracting KPIs from raw financial documents and a Beneish M-Score based fraud detection module, a Sentiment Analysis Module with FinBERT for scoring the management tone and hedging language in earnings transcripts, and a Predictive Signal Engine using XGBoost with SHAP explainability to predict buy/sell/hold signals correlated with stock price movement over yfinance. The Conversational Q&A Module utilizes the Claude API for natural language document queries.

It is developed using the Next.js Frontend, FastAPI Backend, PostgreSQL Database and scikit-learn pipelines, deployed by Vercel and Railway. FinWhisper makes financial intelligence accessible to retail investors, small and medium enterprises (SMEs) and analysts, and automates the entire financial workflow from document ingestion to explainable, actionable insights.
